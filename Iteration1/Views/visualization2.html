Despite popular belief, children are one of the largest groups of Australians experiencing homelessness. In fact, children under the age of 18 make up 27% of people experiencing homelessness. And this figure is likely to be an undercount due to the limitations of the Census as a vehicle for capturing people aged 12-18 who are staying temporarily with friends and relatives.
