  • About

Experiencing homelessness has an impact on both the physical and mental health of children. In addition, children who are experiencing homelessness have been found to have less access to health services due to increased mobility, lack of transport and financial difficulties. Immunisation among children who are experiencing homelessness is low and leads to increased risk of infectious diseases.
